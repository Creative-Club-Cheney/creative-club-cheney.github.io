Creative Club is an extra-curricular club for S1-2 based at Aberdeen Grammar School, Aberdeen. From comics to coding and stories to drawings, anything goes at this club - as long as its creative! This website showcases some of the fantastic projects made by our members. The first Creative Club was founded by 5 people, names anonymous, who wanted to make a club in cheney. We are now expanding to new horizons, 500 miles across the UK
